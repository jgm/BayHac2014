# Here is some math

$$
\lim_{h\to0} \frac{\sin(x+h) - \sin(x)}{h} = \cos x .
$$
Also $\limsup_{n\to\infty} a_n$.

$$
   \nabla \cdot \vec{v} =
   \frac{\partial v_x}{\partial x} +
   \frac{\partial v_y}{\partial y} +
   \frac{\partial v_z}{\partial z}
